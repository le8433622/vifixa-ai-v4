# One Page Pitch

## Vifixa AI

**Smart services for real life.**

## Problem

Repair and maintenance services are fragmented, inconsistent and difficult to trust. Customers struggle to find reliable workers, understand fair pricing and track warranty. Workers struggle to find stable demand, prove skill and manage jobs professionally.

## Solution

Vifixa AI is an AI-powered service operations platform for repair, maintenance and home/business services. Customers describe a problem using text, images or video. AI diagnoses the issue, estimates pricing, matches the right worker and tracks the entire service journey.

## Product

- Mobile app for customers, workers and admins.
- AI diagnosis and price estimation.
- Worker matching and dispatch.
- Order tracking and status events.
- Before/after media proof.
- Warranty and complaint management.
- Trust score and audit logs.

## Market

Initial market: urban home repair and maintenance.  
Expansion: property management, facility maintenance, business chains, equipment warranties, service workforce automation.

## Business Model

- Commission per completed order.
- Subscription plans for households.
- Worker tools and premium features.
- B2B SaaS for maintenance operations.
- Marketplace for parts and supplies.
- Extended warranty and insurance partnerships.

## Why Now

AI can turn messy natural-language requests, images and service histories into structured workflows. Mobile adoption and on-demand service behavior make the market ready for an AI-native service platform.

## Vision

To become the AI operating system for real-world services.
